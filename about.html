Apni Poetry

Welcome to AapniPoetry, myself Ayush Anand from Patna Bihar,
Actually i'm not a shayar
bas jab dil karta hai thoda bahut likh leta hoon.
It's 2019 when i was standing in my balcony
and thinking something about what's going on with me
and that night there was a little wind,
then i wrote something for the first time -
"Hum jo likh rahe the seene me afsos khud ka kamini hawa
sab mitati chali gayi..."
And from there i started writing something relatable,
Kehte hai na sbki life me kuch na kuch kahani hoti
hai bas aapko dikhni chahiye
aur yeh tabhi ho pata hai jab
aap khud ke bare mein sochna start karte hai
so we will start thinking about ourselves from today and now.
